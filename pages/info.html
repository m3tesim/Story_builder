How To Play?

The story game is a fun group activity which develops each player's storytelling skills. This game is best played between 2 to 8 players. The aim of the game is to create a complete story as a team. Going around group of players where each player should contribute one sentence to the story at a time .

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to play the game:

  1. Gather your players online.
  2. Person one, starts off the story.
  3. Person two continues the story by saying the next sentence.
  4. Person three carries the story on and so on.
  5. Depending on the size of the group each player may have between 2 to 5 goes each.
  6. When the story is coming to an end, the last player says the ending sentence.

Story Game Rules & Tips :

There are not many rules in this game, as the goal of this game is to expand imagination and have fun. However, there are some tips to make sure the game goes smoothly:

  1. You need at least two players in the game
  2. The game can be as long as you like or as short as you like.
  3. Make sure you go around Players, so each player has equal turns.
  4. To make the game more challenging for players, you might want to set a time limit or a limit on how many turns each player has.
  5. You could also make the game more challenging by introducing 'special words' that the players must use in their sentences.
